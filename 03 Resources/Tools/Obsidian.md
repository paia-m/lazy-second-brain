# Obsidian

URL: https://obsidian.md

Obsidian is the app used to open this vault as a local markdown workspace.

Related docs: [[START_HERE]], [[SETUP]], [[VAULT_MAP]], and [[03 Resources/PARA Method|PARA Method]].

## Why It Matters

It lets you browse, edit, link, and search notes without locking them into a proprietary format.

## Manual Step

Download Obsidian, install it, then choose "Open folder as vault" and select this repo folder.

After opening the vault, view [[VAULT_MAP]] or graph view to see the seeded links.
