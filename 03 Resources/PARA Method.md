# PARA Method

PARA means Projects, Areas, Resources, Archive.

This vault adds `00 Inbox` in front of PARA so beginners have one obvious place for new notes.

Related docs: [[START_HERE]], [[SETUP]], and [[VAULT_MAP]].

## Folders

- `00 Inbox`: new notes before you know where they belong.
- `01 Projects`: active work with a finish line.
- `02 Areas`: ongoing responsibilities.
- `03 Resources`: useful references.
- `04 Archive`: finished, paused, or inactive material.

Capture first. Move notes later.

Tools that support this structure: [[03 Resources/Tools/Obsidian|Obsidian]], [[03 Resources/Tools/Graphify|Graphify]], and [[03 Resources/Tools/QMD|QMD]].
